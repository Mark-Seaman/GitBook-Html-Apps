### Knowledge is the currency
Build systems to capture what you know

### Capturing Information
* Systematically record
* Organize as needed
* Build a process for retrieval

### Basic Tools
* One Note
* Google Keep
* Evernote - my favorite

### Required features
* Quick capture (5 seconds)
* Quick recall (5 seconds)
* Folders
* Tags
* Mobile access
