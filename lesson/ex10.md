## HTML Entities
Use the following somewhere appropriate on your page

* &amp;amp; - Ampersand
* &amp;copy; - Copyright
* &amp;lt; - Less than
* &amp;gt; - Greater than
* &amp;nbsp; - Non-blocking space
* &amp;quot; - Double quote
* &amp;apos; - Single quote
